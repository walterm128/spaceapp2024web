Galactic Games: Fun in a Microgravity Environment!

Zero-G Playgrounds

By Team Nerdonauts

The challenge of life on a space station is clear: long hours of work in confined spaces, surrounded by neutral colors, all in microgravity conditions. Although the space environment is unique, it can lead to isolation and physical and mental fatigue. Astronauts need exercise, mental stimulation and a break from routine.

Our solution: AR augmented reality and VR virtual reality games designed specifically for microgravity. We have created physical and mental activities that take advantage of the unique conditions of space. Tailored sports, logic challenges and relaxation exercises keep astronauts physically and mentally fit.

These games allow them to connect with the crew, other space stations and family members on Earth, as well as offering moments of personal disconnection. Microgravity becomes an advantage, transforming the space routine into an interactive, healthy and fun experience.

ZeroG Playgrounds

Relax

The objective of space relaxation is to reduce the astronaut's stress by simulating VR environments such as:

  • Forest (this also allows you to simulate a walking exercise and also has an option to fly through the VR environment). Aero Yoga can also be practiced in this environment.
  • Watch a movie in a terrestrial environment created with VR.
Logic

While logic games, are mental exercises such as chess or drawing with gestures, they work through augmented reality AR, such as:

  • Drawing with gestures
  • Chess
  • AR Pictionary
Sports

And finally sports consisting of the following spaces:

  • Rowing: which can be individual or cooperative with the crew. The way of rowing is detected by kinetic bracelets. This is a VR environment.
  • Basketball: which can be played as AR and is activated by viewing with the glasses the specific QR code.

Physical condition

Minimize effects on the body due to lack of or micro gravity, such as:
Loss of bone mass and muscle mass

Psychological help

Minimize the effects of stress
